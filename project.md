# All In at the End

## Project Overview

Genre: Roguelike / Gambling / Narrative  
Theme: Death, chance, regret

All In at the End is a narrative-driven roguelike where the player wakes up in a casino run by Death.

Instead of gambling money, the player bets pieces of their soul—memories, emotions, and years of their life.

Each gambling table represents a different mechanic including:

- Slots
- Poker
- Roulette
- Dice

Winning extends your life. Losing permanently alters your character’s personality, dialogue options, abilities, and ending.

The game combines chance-based systems with permanent narrative consequences.

## Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge:** Making gambling feel meaningful rather than random.

**Solution:** Losses affect the player’s soul, altering narrative paths and gameplay instead of simply reducing stats.

**Challenge:** Balancing fairness with emotional tension.

**Solution:** Probability systems adjust slightly based on the player’s soul condition.
