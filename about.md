## Joshua Villamar

Hello! My name is Joshua Villamar and I am an aspiring game developer with an interest in gameplay systems and narrative design.

I enjoy creating games where mechanics and story interact with each other, allowing player decisions to shape the experience.

### Skills

- Unity Game Development
- C# Programming
- Game System Design
- Narrative Design
- GitHub Version Control
- Game Project Planning

### Interests

I enjoy studying how mechanics affect player psychology, especially in games that create tension and risk. Roguelikes and decision-heavy games are a major influence on my design approach.

One of my favorite games is Hollow Knight because of its atmosphere, exploration, and world design.

Outside of development I enjoy volleyball, anime, and exploring different game mechanics.
